# AI-Powered-Lead-Generation-System

## Overview

The **AI-Powered Lead Generation System** is a Salesforce-based application designed to automate the process of identifying, scoring, and prioritizing potential customers. By leveraging **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Salesforce Agentforce**, the system analyzes customer information, website interactions, and other business data to predict which leads are most likely to convert into customers.

The application helps sales teams focus on high-value opportunities, improves lead management efficiency, and supports faster, data-driven decision-making.

## Features

* Automated lead generation from customer and business data.
* AI-powered lead scoring and conversion prediction.
* Intelligent lead prioritization based on conversion probability.
* Real-time alerts and recommendations for sales representatives.
* Automated workflows using Salesforce Flow and Agentforce.
* Lead management through Salesforce CRM.
* Reports and dashboards for sales performance analysis.

## Technologies Used

* Salesforce CRM
* Salesforce Agentforce
* Salesforce Flow
* Apex
* AI-Based Lead Scoring
* Reports & Dashboards
* Custom Objects and Validation Rules

## Project Objectives

* Automate the lead identification process.
* Reduce manual effort in lead qualification.
* Improve sales productivity through AI-driven insights.
* Increase lead conversion rates by prioritizing high-potential prospects.
* Provide actionable recommendations to sales representatives.

## System Workflow

1. Collect customer and website interaction data.
2. Store lead information in Salesforce.
3. AI analyzes lead behavior and business data.
4. Agentforce predicts lead conversion probability.
5. Leads are automatically scored and prioritized.
6. Sales representatives receive alerts and recommendations.
7. Follow-up actions are tracked and monitored through Salesforce.
